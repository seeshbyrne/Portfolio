About

As a junior software engineer completing an Immersive Bootcamp at General Assembly, I bring a unique blend of creativity, logical thinking, and business acumen to the tech industry. With a bachelor's degree in business from Adelaide University and a diverse background in e-commerce, customer service, administration, and social media, I excel at bringing innovative ideas to life. My passion lies in front-end development, where I can leverage my design skills and creativity to create engaging user experiences.


I love design and getting more creative, combining that with business, logic, and problem-solving. From a young age, I enjoyed designing websites on Wix and drawing things on paper to see if I could create digital versions to show people. I thrive on connecting with others and building things that make people happy. I am eager to contribute to a dynamic team where I can utilize my diverse skill set and continue to grow in the tech industry.
